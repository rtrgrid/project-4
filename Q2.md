## Q2. What problem does Helios's composable sleep trigger system solve compared to hard-coded timing logic?

### Answer
Helios's composable sleep trigger system solves the problem of **waiting for many different kinds of conditions without baking fixed timing rules into the agent loop**. Hard-coded timing logic can only express simple waits like 'sleep 5 minutes' or rigid polling intervals. Helios instead supports timer, process-exit, metric, file, resource, and user-message conditions, and it can combine them with **AND/OR logic**.

This means the agent can wake when the *right thing* happens, not just after a fixed delay. That is much more flexible for ML workflows where jobs may finish early, metrics may hit thresholds, files may appear, or GPUs may free up.
